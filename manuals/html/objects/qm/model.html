Shin-Metiu model

The 1D charge transfer model system proposed by Shin and Metiu [15] is implemented.

\[\begin{split}\hat{H}_{BO}(r;R) = -\frac{1}{2}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial r^2} +\frac{1}{|\frac{L}{2}-R|}&+\frac{1}{|\frac{L}{2}+R|}\nonumber\\ -\frac{\text{erf}\left(|R-r|/R_c\right)}{|R-r|} -\frac{\text{erf}\left(|r-\frac{L}{2}|/R_r\right)}{|r-\frac{L}{2}|} &-\frac{\text{erf}\left(|r+\frac{L}{2}|/R_l\right)}{|r+\frac{L}{2}|}\end{split}\]

The unit of parameters is atomic unit.
